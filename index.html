Course Details

  • Pg-DAC
Post Graduate Diploma in Advanced Computing (PG-DAC) is the flagship programme of ACTS which focuses engineers and IT professionals who would wish to venture into the domain of advanced computing. It aims at enabling students to work on current technology scenarios and prepare them to keep pace with the changes in technology and requirements of the growing IT industry. The depth and width of the course is unique to a wide spectrum of requirements of the ICT industry. Running successfully for over seventeen years, the PG-DAC course has yielded more than 4000 students, with pleasant positions in the industry today. Expert Industry Faculty & Consultants who train students in the best possible manner suiting industry requirements. Outstanding industry interactions to keep students updated with the latest trends in IT. Consistent and Excellent Placement Record. Best available Infrastructure, with Air-conditioned Classrooms and a fully equipped Computer Lab to promote professional learning. Apart from prescribed syllabus & languages, MET also provides a strong foundation of concepts like Distributed Computing in Java (RMI-IIOP)/.Net, Technology & Code Integration, Implementation of RIA & Design Patterns, IA32 Assembly & Python and so on. It also concentrates on specialised training sessions on Hibernate/Struts that match Industry Requirements. The Project and Practical based training delivery model which enables students to be factually proficient, the Placement Preparation Module which includes Rigorous Aptitude Preparations, helps in Training on Communication & Personality Development, Thorough Interview preparations through Mock Interview sessions from Alumni and Industry Associates, play an added advantage. Personalised attention in Class rooms and Labs.

Course Details

  • Pg-DBDA
The theoretical and practical mix of the Post Graduate Diploma in Big Data Analytics (PG-DBDA) programme has the following focus: To explore the fundamental concepts of big data analytics with in-depth knowledge and understanding of the big data analytics domain To understand the various search methods and visualization techniques and to use various techniques for mining data stream To analyze and solve problems conceptually and practically from diverse industries, such as government manufacturing, retail, education, banking/ finance, healthcare and pharmaceutical To undertake consulting and industrial projects with significant data analysis component for better understanding of the theoretical concepts from statistics, economics and building future solutions data analytics to make an impact in the technological advancement To use advanced analytical tools/ decision-making tools/ operation research techniques to analyze the complex problems and get ready to develop such new techniques for the future To learn Cloud Computing, accessing resources and services needed to perform functions with dynamically changing needs

  • Pg-DITIS
The Post Graduate Diploma in IT Infrastructure,Systems and Security (PG-DITISS) is a fulltime postgraduate course comprising of 9 Compulsory Modules and a Project. Qualification: 1. Graduate in Engineering (10+2+4 or 10+3+3 years) in IT / Computer Science / Electronics / Telecommunications / Electrical / Instrumentation, OR 2. MSc/MS (10+2+3+2 years) in Computer Science, IT, Electronics with Mathematics in 10+2, OR 3. Post Graduate in Mathematics / Statistics, OR 4. MCA Note: The candidates must have secured a minimum of 55% marks in their qualifying examination A candidate seeking admission to PG-DITISS course must appear for SECTION A and B test papers of C-CAT 2022.
